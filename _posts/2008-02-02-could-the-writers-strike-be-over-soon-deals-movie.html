The writers strike may be over. Rumors are swirling around Hollywood about a deal between the Writers Guild of America and the Alliance of Motion Pictures and Television Producers.

Further adding to the speculation is a tweet by Xeni Jardin saying the strike is amost over. Her friends say as soon as early next week. Xeni is a professional journalist. I trust what she says. She writes for BoingBoing and Wired.

The New York Times reported today that a deal may be near after two weeks of closed negotiations.
