The tomato count begins. Neighbor said: “You should count the tomatoes you pick. I bet you have 1,000.”

He may be correct. At season’s end, we tend to have target contests with those tomatoes rotten and not so great for eating. The result: lots of tomato plants in places you’d never expect.
