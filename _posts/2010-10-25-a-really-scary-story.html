After the IBM opening this morning, I left through a ground floor entrance. Everyone else climbed the arena stairs. I did not want to climb the stairs. I found an elevator and took it to the third floor. IT WAS REALLY DARK AND QUIET UP THERE. IT WAS KIND OF SCARY.

I wandered for what seemed like miles. I took an elevator to the first floor.

I live another day.
